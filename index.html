FAQ

Answers, before you ask.

Didn't find what you're looking for? Send us a question →

How long does a typical migration take?

For most stores, 2 to 48 hours of migration time. Large catalogs (50k+ SKUs) or complex B2B setups can run up to a week. We give you an exact ETA after the free demo migration — no guessing.

Will my SEO rankings survive the move?

Yes. We generate 301 redirects for every product, collection, blog post and page URL — and migrate meta titles, descriptions and image alt text exactly as they were. Most merchants see no measurable ranking drop.

Can I keep selling on my old store during migration?

Absolutely. Your old store stays live the entire time. New orders that come in during migration are synced over via delta sync before launch — no manual export, no missed customers.

What if something goes wrong?

Every migration ships with a 7-day rollback window — one click reverses everything. Plus, your source data is never touched. If we can't deliver a clean migration, we refund you in full.

Do you migrate BigCommerce stores?

BigCommerce migrations are launching shortly. Join the waitlist to be one of the first 50 merchants on board — and get 20% off your migration.

I have a custom-built store. Can you still help?

Yes. Our Enterprise team handles custom platforms, headless setups and exotic data shapes regularly. Tell us about your stack and we'll scope a tailored migration in 24 hours.
